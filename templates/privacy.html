Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Service — to display your expense history, generate summaries, and power the features you use. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and expense records are permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. No other third-party services are embedded in the app.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on the site. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
